FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account on UP-NEXUS?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Get Started" button, choose your role (Startup, Investor, Mentor, etc.), fill in your details, and you're ready to go. The basic account is completely free.

Is UP-NEXUS free to use?

Yes! We offer a free Freemium plan that gives you access to core features. For advanced features like priority visibility and verified badges, check our Premium plans starting at 1200 DA/year.

How can I get my profile verified?

Verification is available for Premium users. Once you upgrade, our team will review your profile and documents within 48 hours. Verified profiles get a badge and higher visibility in search results.

Can I post job opportunities?

Absolutely! You can post announcements including job openings, funding opportunities, events, and more. Single announcements cost 1000 DA, or you can get unlimited posts with a Premium subscription.

How do I connect with investors?

Browse our Ecosystem directory, filter by "Investors", and view their profiles. You can send connection requests to start a conversation. Premium members get priority in investor matches.

What support do you offer?

We offer email support for all users, with priority support for Premium members. Our Help Center has guides and tutorials. You can also reach us on social media for quick questions.
