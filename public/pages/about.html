About UP-NEXUS

Building the bridge between Algeria's entrepreneurial dreams and their realization

Our Story

UP-NEXUS was born from a simple observation: Algeria's entrepreneurial ecosystem is rich with talent, innovation, and ambition, but fragmented across countless platforms, groups, and networks.

We envisioned a unified digital hub where startups could find investors, where mentors could discover emerging talent, and where the entire ecosystem could collaborate seamlessly. A place where every entrepreneur, regardless of their stage, could find the resources and connections they need to succeed.

Today, UP-NEXUS serves as that bridge - connecting the dots in Algeria's startup landscape and accelerating the growth of our nation's innovation economy.

Our Values

What Drives Us

The principles that guide everything we do at UP-NEXUS

Connection

We believe that the right connection at the right time can transform ideas into impactful businesses. We're obsessed with making those connections happen.

Accessibility

Entrepreneurship should be accessible to everyone. We're committed to removing barriers and democratizing access to resources and opportunities.

Trust

We build trust through verification, transparency, and accountability. Every actor on our platform is vetted to ensure a high-quality ecosystem.

Innovation

We practice what we preach. We continuously innovate our platform to better serve the evolving needs of Algeria's entrepreneurial community.

Community

We're building more than a platform - we're nurturing a community where entrepreneurs support each other and celebrate collective wins.

Local Pride

We're proudly Algerian, building for Algeria. We understand local challenges and opportunities like no one else can.
